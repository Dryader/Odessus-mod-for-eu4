# Odessus Mod for Europa Universalis IV

A historical modification for Europa Universalis IV that focuses on the Battle of Varna period and the Balkans region, with enhanced gameplay for Bulgaria and surrounding nations.

## Overview

The Odessus mod provides an alternative historical experience starting from **May 29, 1445** (after the Battle of Varna), with a special focus on the Kingdom of Bulgaria and the complex political situation in the Balkans following the crusade. This mod reimagines the post-Varna period with enhanced missions, events, and historical accuracy for the region.

## Features

### New Start Date
- **1445.5.29** - Alternative start date focusing on the aftermath of the Battle of Varna
- Modified bookmark "The Last Crusade" replacing the standard "Rise of the Ottomans"

### Bulgaria Enhancements
- Complete rework of Bulgarian national missions
- New Bulgarian-specific events including:
  - Victory of Varna alternative history events
  - Bulgarian succession and heir events
  - Special decisions for Bulgaria's expansion
- Revised Bulgarian national ideas (modified idea 2)
- New government types:
  - Bulgarian Tsardom (modified)
  - Bulgarian Kralstvo (new)
- Tarnovo set as historical capital

### Historical Changes

#### Diplomacy & Alliances
- Modified Poland-Hungary personal union (until 1446)
- Poland-Croatia union
- Poland-Lithuania changed from personal union to alliance in 1445
- Removed Ottoman vassalization of Wallachia in 1445
- Removed Ottoman guarantee of Ragusa
- Removed Venetian guarantee of Albania
- Removed Polish guarantee of Moldavia

- Crusade of Varna war file modifications

### Province Changes

- Ottoman flavor events for the region
- Byzantine missions related to the Bulgarian sphere

## Installation

1. Download or clone this repository
2. Place the mod folder in your Europa Universalis IV mod directory:
   - **Windows**: `Documents\Paradox Interactive\Europa Universalis IV\mod\`
   - **Linux**: `~/.local/share/Paradox Interactive/Europa Universalis IV/mod/`
   - **Mac**: `~/Documents/Paradox Interactive/Europa Universalis IV/mod/`
3. Launch Europa Universalis IV
4. Enable the Odessus mod in the launcher
5. Start a new game with "The Last Crusade" bookmark (1445 start date)

## Compatibility

- Designed for Europa Universalis IV
- Compatible with the base game and most major DLCs
- May conflict with other mods that heavily modify the Balkans region or 1444/1445 start dates

## Recommended Nations to Play

- **Bulgaria** - Experience the full reworked mission tree and unique events
- **Poland** - Lead the aftermath of the Varna crusade with the new alliance structure
- **Hungary** - Navigate the personal union with Poland
- **Ottomans** - Face a more challenging Balkans situation
- **Byzantium** - Attempt restoration with modified diplomatic landscape
